Information for International Customers

International orders ship via UPS Worldwide Expedited service. Generally, shipments are delivered within 4 business days of shipment. UPS will ship your computer to the nearest customs office. Once customs clears the package, you will be contacted for delivery authorization to your residence or place of business. UPS Worldwide Expedited service includes shipment and insurance. System76, Inc. does not collect taxes or duties for orders being shipped internationally. To determine taxes or duties owed, if any, please contact your customs office with both the description of your order and its total price. All System76 prices are in US Dollar (USD). System76 manufactures US keyboard layouts on all laptops. You can choose your own language and software-based layout during the first time-user setup. Keyboard stickers may be used to adjust to your specific language. System76 products are compatible with world wide power grids. You may need to purchase a plug adapter for your computer.

General Shipping Information

Exact shipping, handling, and insurance costs are calculated at checkout. Our laptops, desktops, servers, and keyboards are custom built for each order, unless otherwise specified, and generally ship out within 10 business days after placing an order. Once an order has shipped, you will be notified via email of the tracking information. We will notify you if for any reason your order is delayed.
